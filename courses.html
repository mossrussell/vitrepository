Practical Vine Physiology

This course is a review of vine phyisology. This is a physiology course made by a grapegrower for grapegrowers. The course consists of 13 hours of videos made by me and supplementary material from other sources. I reccomend watching my videos at 1.25X the original speed. You will also find quizzes that follow up on the material from each class session. Feel free to email me for answers to the quizzes and exams.
